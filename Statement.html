Where am I?

Artist Statment

By: Kiana Greenwood

October 11, 2024





This project named "Where am I" is based around the feeling of being lost, confused and isolated. You dont know where your going or what you'll find. I used movement as a way for you to explore the pages and find the hidden texts and links throughout. Some lead you on and others bring you back to the beginning. I interpreted the theme of location as not knowing your own. Your placed in this site without any backstory or lead. I wanted to create something simplistic in design and very retro based. I explored different techniques to create new feelings as you scroll through and move around the different pages. Overall creating a site making you want to check all the corners and every pixel throughout. Something artistic but with limited visuals so your relying more on the text throughout .In the future I would further this project by creating more interactions and adding more pages linked through to lead you on a a longer exploration.
